💡 Pro-Tip: The "Up-Arrow" Shortcut
In Termux, you don't need to type node index.js every time. Just tap the Up Arrow on your keyboard (or use the shortcut Ctrl + P) to cycle through your previous commands.

📂 How to Open the Output
Once the terminal says 🚀 BUILD SUCCESS, you can't open the Word file inside Termux. Instead:

Open your tablet's "My Files" or "Files" app.

Go to the project folder.

Tap Geological_Field_Journal_2026.docx.

It will open in Microsoft Word or Google Docs for a final review.
